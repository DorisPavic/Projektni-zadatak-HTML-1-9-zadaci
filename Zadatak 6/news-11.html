Covid-19 information

Latest information about corona virus

In the Dubrovnik-Neretva County, 41 new cases of coronavirus infection were recorded in the last 24 hours.

These are 24 males and 17 females: 16 from Dubrovnik, 8 from Orebić, four from Konavle and Vela Luka, 3 from Korčula, two from Lumbarda and the Dubrovnik Parish, and one each from Smokvica and Ston.

174 people were cured: 69 from Dubrovnik, 19 from Metković and Vela Luka, 16 from Župa dubrovačka, 12 from Konavle, 11 from Orebić, 8 from Ploče, 7 from Opuzen, 5 from Ston, 3 from Korčula and one person from Blato , Dubrovnik coast, Slivno, Smokvica is Mljet.

In the last 24 hours, 227 samples were processed, and since the beginning of the pandemic, a total of 163,724 samples have been analyzed.

65 people tested positive for coronavirus were hospitalized in OB Dubrovnik, nine patients required intensive care and were on a respirator.

There are 1597 people in self-isolation, and in the last 24 hours no violation of the self-isolation measure was recorded. Since the beginning of the pandemic, a total of 975 cases of self-isolation violations have been identified.

The headquarters of the CZ DNŽ continues to appeal to the citizens to adhere to all prescribed measures by the Croatian Institute of Public Health and the Civil Protection Headquarters of the Republic of Croatia.

19 November 2021

Source: 24sata.hr
