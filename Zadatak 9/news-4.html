Kono the turtle is no longer with us

Kono has died

Yesterday afternoon we found out that Kono the turtle has died, her life was extinguished by a ship's propeller!

Turtle Kono was well known to everyone, our dearest guest who always returned faithfully and always attracted attention from all sides. Our beloved turtle we are sad that you had to leave us in this way. You were our dearest guest, and we were looking forward to your every arrival!

Also we found out today that another turtle was seen in the Lovište bay this morning and that makes us very happy!
